COVID-19 Live Tracker is a subdomain maintained by Untap'd, a startup from Sikkim, India, in the Connected Cars Domain. At the time of the global pandemic of COVID-19 and Lockdown, it is important to share information with others and that is exactly what we are doing. We collect data from various renowned sources and the backend verifies it and displays the most credible data to the viewers. We do not intend to gain anything out of this. Our sole purpose has been to help the world come out of this as soon as possible. For that, we need to share awareness and this is how we have decided to step up.
